Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I do powerlifting and MMA?

There are a multitude of reasons someone would want to do both a fighting class and a weightlifting class. The fighting class will increase flexibility, reaction time, and hand eye coordination which are all traits an athlete wants to improve. The powerlifting class will then give you the strength to enhance your speed, power, and overall strength in sports. By doing both at the same time could give an athlete an edge over the competition who doesn’t train as vigorously.

Can I use the gym membership to workout like any normal gym?

Yes you can. What we hope to have happen by joining our powerlifting program is that you consider our trainers advice but you don’t have to. Instead you can just use the equipment as you like while still having good gym etiquette to our other members.
