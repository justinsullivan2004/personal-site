What is MMA and Powerlifting?

What is MMA and PowerLifting? Our gym offers two services, MMA and powerlifting but what are they? Well, MMA stands for mixed martial arts so you will be learning to wrestle, grapple, and other fighting techniques taught by our trained professionals and practice skills that you learn as a class and in groups. There will also be competitions we enter as a gym with our top fighters for each weight class entering, if they want to, to bring a competitive side of our training into our gym.

Powerlifting is a strength sport that focuses on three lifts; squat, bench, and deadlift. With the powerlifting membership you will be given a personal trainer that will help give you a personal workout plan tailored to you to see the best gains. Similar to our MMA program, we will also have powerlifting competitions based on weight class if customers choose to enter.
